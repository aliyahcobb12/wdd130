Overview

Purpose

Finding a study spot can be hard, but this website has all the information you need to find the perfect spot on campus for your needs. Whether you prefer silent libraries with minimal distractions, or lively ambiances with space for the occasional brain break, this site will help you find the perfect place on campus to sit down and get focused, even some of BYU-Idaho's most hidden treasures.
